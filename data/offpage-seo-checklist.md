# GoModexa Off-Page SEO Checklist

This checklist covers the off-page work that must happen outside the website.

Important:
- Off-page SEO cannot be fully "implemented" only by code changes.
- The site can support off-page SEO with stronger entity signals and a reference page.
- Real off-page strength comes from citations, backlinks, business listings, creator mentions, and brand profiles.

## 1. First Setup

Complete these first:
- Create or update Google Search Console ownership
- Submit `https://www.gomodexa.com/sitemap.xml`
- Create Google Business Profile if eligible
- Create consistent brand profiles:
  - Instagram
  - Facebook
  - LinkedIn
  - YouTube
  - Pinterest
- Add those live profile URLs into `.env.local` using:
  - `NEXT_PUBLIC_INSTAGRAM_URL`
  - `NEXT_PUBLIC_FACEBOOK_URL`
  - `NEXT_PUBLIC_LINKEDIN_URL`
  - `NEXT_PUBLIC_YOUTUBE_URL`
  - `NEXT_PUBLIC_X_URL`
  - `NEXT_PUBLIC_PINTEREST_URL`

## 2. Citation Consistency

Use the same brand details everywhere:
- Brand name: `GoModexa`
- Website: `https://www.gomodexa.com`
- Email: `supporttrustrove@gmail.com`
- Phone: `+91 9082670335`
- Service area: `India`
- Reference page: `https://www.gomodexa.com/brand-resources`

## 3. Directory Submission Targets

Submit the brand to:
- Google Business Profile
- Bing Places
- Apple Business Connect if relevant
- Indian business directories
- Ecommerce store directories
- Startup/company profile sites
- Coupon/deal listing profiles if brand-safe

For each submission:
- Use the exact same business info
- Link to `/brand-resources`
- Link to `/about-us`
- Link to `/contact-us`

## 4. Backlink Targets

Priority targets:
- Product review bloggers
- Gift guide blogs
- Home and kitchen bloggers
- Beauty and lifestyle bloggers
- Deal and shopping recommendation sites
- Small media/news mentions
- Affiliate creators and link roundups

Anchor text mix:
- `GoModexa`
- `GoModexa online store`
- `GoModexa brand resources`
- natural URL anchors

Avoid:
- repeating exact-match keyword anchors too aggressively

## 6. Social Profile Checklist

Every profile should include:
- brand name
- short brand description
- website URL
- link to `/brand-resources`
- matching logo
- matching contact email if appropriate

Post types to support off-page SEO:
- new arrivals
- category highlights
- blog article snippets
- gifting edits
- buyer guides
- trust/policy snippets

## 7. Monthly Off-Page Targets

Try to achieve per month:
- 10 to 20 directory/citation updates
- 5 to 10 outreach emails
- 2 to 5 brand mentions or backlinks
- 8 to 12 social posts linking to site content
- 2 collaboration attempts with niche creators/bloggers

## 8. What To Track

Track in a sheet:
- source site
- profile/listing URL
- submitted date
- live date
- linked page
- backlink type
- follow / nofollow
- notes

## 9. Fastest Wins

Do these first:
- add real social profile URLs to env and schema
- publish Google Business Profile
- submit to 10 business directories
- outreach to 5 niche blogs
- share every new blog on social profiles
